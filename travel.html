Wulan-Butong Grassland

Geographical Location: Inner Mongolia, China

Wulan-Butong Grassland is a vast stretch of pristine landscape located in Inner Mongolia. It's a tapestry of vivid green grass, clear blue skies, and livestock that graze freely, painting a serene picture of nature's beauty. The grassland is especially magical in the morning when the golden rays of the sun illuminate the dew-kissed grass, creating a spectacle that's nothing short of poetic.

Visitors often find themselves mesmerized by the simple yet profound beauty of the place. From the gentle breeze that carries tales of ancient traditions to the harmonious relationship between the livestock and their surroundings, Wulan-Butong offers a tranquil escape from the bustling city life.

ZhangBei, Hebei

Geographical Location: ZhangBei, Hebei, China

ZhangBei, located in Hebei, presents an incredible juxtaposition of natural beauty with human-made marvels. While the vast grasslands paint a picture of tranquility, the mammoth electricity generator fans and poles stand as a testament to human ingenuity. These giants, spread across the landscape, generate clean energy while accentuating the panoramic view of the grasslands.

The region is not just about grasslands and generator fans. Spiritual symbols like Buddhist flags fluttering in the wind add a unique cultural and spiritual dimension to this picturesque setting. At night, the place transforms into a celestial wonder with stars painting the sky, making it a haven for stargazers.

Biei, Hokkaido, Japan

Geographical Location: Biei, Hokkaido, Japan

Biei, situated in Hokkaido, Japan, is an exquisite town renowned for its scenic vistas, comprising picturesque trees and the ethereal Aoiike (Blue Pond). This town radiates an air of tranquility, with its iconic trees and dreamy landscapes of Aoiike, making it a must-visit destination for nature lovers and photographers alike.

The modernized and lightened versions of Aoiike offer a dreamy and almost surreal view, playing with light and shadows to give visitors different perspectives of the same beauty. Then there's the world-famous tree, which has graced numerous advertisements and has become synonymous with the charm of Biei.

Mt. North Star, Naka Furano, Hokkaido, Japan

Geographical Location: Mt. North Star, Naka Furano, Hokkaido, Japan

Mount North Star in Naka Furano is a captivating destination, particularly celebrated for its breathtaking sea of clouds and enchanting sunrises. The mountain and its surrounding vistas offer an unparalleled experience, where nature's beauty reveals itself in an array of mesmerizing moments - from the hush before dawn to the radiant glow of the sun piercing through the clouds.

One cannot help but marvel at the villages nestled amidst these clouds, their ethereal beauty accentuated against the backdrop of mountains. The sun, peeking through mountain peaks and casting its golden rays on the ground, adds to the magical allure of this destination.
